🎉 Epic Confetti Celebration

🚀 Epic Achievement Feature:
When enabled, users who reach the epic threshold get an epic physics-based confetti celebration! Confetti particles accumulate at the bottom of the screen and can be disturbed by:
  • Mobile: Tilt and shake your device (accelerometer)
  • Desktop: Drag your mouse through the pile
  • Gentle shake: Disturbs settled particles in the pile
Note: This is in addition to the regular confetti celebration at the regular threshold.
